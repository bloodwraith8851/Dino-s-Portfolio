# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters and Hiring Managers. They need to quickly understand Rakesh's skills, experience, and the quality of his work in a few seconds.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a high-end personal portfolio that showcases Rakesh Sarkar as a Developer, Designer, and GenAI Engineer. Success looks like an interface that immediately conveys technical excellence and design taste, leading to job opportunities and interviews.

## Brand Personality
Dark, cinematic, premium. The interface should feel highly polished, intentional, and technically extraordinary.

## Anti-references
- A generic SaaS template
- A plain boring text resume
- An overly chaotic WebGL experiment

## Design Principles
- **Show, don't tell**: Demonstrate engineering and design skills through the interface itself (smooth motion, perfect alignment).
- **Expert confidence**: The design should feel restrained but deliberate. No unnecessary noise, only purposeful elements.
- **Cinematic depth**: Use lighting, shadows, and motion to create depth rather than relying on flat cards.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. Accommodations for reduced motion (via `prefers-reduced-motion`), ensuring contrast is maintained even in a dark cinematic theme.
